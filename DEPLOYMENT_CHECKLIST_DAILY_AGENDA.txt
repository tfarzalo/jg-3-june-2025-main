Frequency: Once per day, every day

To change the schedule:
1. Open: supabase/migrations/20260123_setup_daily_cron.sql
2. Edit the cron expression: '0 12 * * *'
3. Unschedule old job: SELECT cron.unschedule('daily-agenda-email-cron');
4. Run the updated migration

Cron format: minute hour day month day-of-week
Examples:
  '0 12 * * *'    = Every day at 12:00 UTC (7:00 AM EST)
  '0 13 * * *'    = Every day at 13:00 UTC (8:00 AM EST)
  '0 14 * * 1-5'  = Weekdays only at 14:00 UTC (9:00 AM EST)
  '0 11 * * *'    = Every day at 11:00 UTC (6:00 AM EST)
